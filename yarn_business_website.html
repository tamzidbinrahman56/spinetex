How It Works

From Source to Your Factory

A clear, transparent process ensures you always know where your yarn is and when it arrives.

1

Enquiry & Quotation

Share your yarn count, quantity, and delivery timeline. We respond with a competitive price within 24 hours.

2

Order Confirmation

Confirm the order with a purchase agreement. We open LC with our banking partner and place the order with the supplier.

3

Production & Shipment

Supplier produces and ships. We track the vessel and handle all export documentation from the source country.

4

Port Clearance

Our C&F agent at Chittagong or Dhaka handles customs clearance, duty payment, and port release efficiently.

5

Warehouse & QC

Yarn arrives at our warehouse. Each lot is checked for count accuracy, moisture, and visual defects before dispatch.

6

Delivery to Factory

Yarn is delivered to your factory gate on the agreed date. Invoice is raised and payment terms apply as agreed.
